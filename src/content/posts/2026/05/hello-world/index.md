Hi! I'm Gareth. I've finally followed through on my longstanding intent to build a personal site. I've wanted,  for a really long time, to have somewhere that I can share my thoughts and own them: as opposed to on a social media site that then gets to stuff ads in between.

Data residency and control over my own image that I put out there, is part of why I wanted this. There is also the creative aspect mixed with the technicality of The World Wide Web™️. I find I am drawn to that space in between twisting the technical knobs and the joy of splashing paint on the canvas.

Anyways, thank you for taking the time. Plenty more to come. Pun intended.
