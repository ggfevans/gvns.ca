Introducing nfs-swiftbar: a plugin for SwiftBar that shows the status of NFS mounts in macOS.

For those unfamiliar, NFS is Network File System: found in Unix-like systems, its the best way to share files over a network. The problem I've found is that macOS can be flaky with using them.

I basically got tired of having to open up a terminal every time my NFS shares acted up, so I threw together (using Claude) a handy little tool to smack 'em until they work.

For the seven people that use macOS and NFS together, you're welcome.

https://github.com/ggfevans/nfs-swiftbar
